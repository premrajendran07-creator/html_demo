A paragraph is a collection of related sentences that deal with a single topic. It serves as a building block of writing, helping to organize thoughts and ideas clearly. Typically, a paragraph begins on a new line and may be indented, marking a pause from the preceding text. The unity and coherence of ideas among sentences are what truly define a paragraph, rather than its length
